Our Story

BookHaven.ph was born from a simple dream: to create a cozy corner where Filipino book lovers could discover their next favorite read. What started as a small collection in a spare room has grown into a beloved online bookstore serving readers across the Philippines.


We believe that every book has the power to transform lives, spark imagination, and connect people across boundaries. Our mission is to make quality books accessible to every Filipino reader.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? ▼
Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days for Metro areas, and 7–14 days for provincial addresses. Expedited shipping is available upon request.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept GCash, PayMaya, Bank Transfer (BDO, BPI, UnionBank), and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible areas.
Can I return or exchange a book? ▼
Yes! We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged or incorrect items. Please contact us at hello@bookhaven.ph with your order number and photos.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders? ▼
Absolutely! For bulk orders of 10 or more copies, please reach out to us directly for special pricing and arrangements.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Yes. All transactions are encrypted and we never store your payment credentials. GCash and PayMaya transactions are protected by their respective platforms.
